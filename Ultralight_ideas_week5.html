3 Ultralight Concept Ideas

  1. Day in Colors Calendar

    2. A calendar to track your overall experience of each day through colors.

    Here is an example: Color Calendar Example


  2. Book Reviews and Suggestions

    3. You can share and rate books that you have read. For each book, rate it on a scale from 1 to 5 and list at least one other book that it reminds you of or that you would recommend to someone who enjoyed the book you listed.

    Here is an example: Book Reviews and Suggestions


  3. Painting Prompts

    4. You can share inspiration for artists to use when starting their next piece. This inspiration can be in any format, including photos, short descriptions, colors, poems, songs, or more.

    Here is an example: Example of Painting Prompts
